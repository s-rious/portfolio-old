Esports is more than just playing video games.

Esports (Electronic Sports) is a term used for competitive video gaming.

Esports is the future of sports

What even is esports?

Esports is the competitive play of Video Games. Those who participate in Esports play, coach, watch, and interact with the competitive play of games of their choice. This interaction is much like traditional sports. Esports dates back to October of 1972 when Stanford University students competed in a video game called "Spacewar". The winner of this competition would win a year long subscription to the Rolling Stones magazine.

What does Esports offer?

Esports is a growing sport. Although not considered an official sport, it still rackets in millions of players globally who wish to compete professionally via video games. Esports offers many opportunities in life, even opening the doors to careers for many around the world. Jobs can include being a professional player or coach. Although often confused with solely gaming, Esports often offers jobs in the field of creating games or an Esports environment.
